Life Cycle of a Butterfly

The butterfly‚Äôs life is a remarkable journey of transformation through four distinct stages. From a tiny egg to a graceful adult, each phase highlights the wonder of nature and metamorphosis.

Egg

The butterfly begins life as a tiny egg, often laid on the underside of leaves.

Caterpillar (Larva)

The caterpillar hatches and feeds voraciously, growing rapidly as it prepares for metamorphosis.

Chrysalis (Pupa)

Inside the chrysalis, the caterpillar transforms completely, developing wings and adult features.

Adult Butterfly

The fully formed butterfly emerges, ready to feed, mate, and continue the cycle of life.
